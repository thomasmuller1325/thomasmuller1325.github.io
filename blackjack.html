Welcome to Blackjack

In Blackjack, players aim to have a hand value as close to 21 as possible without exceeding it, competing against the dealer rather than other players. Number cards are worth their face value, face cards (Jack, Queen, King) are worth 10, and Aces can count as 1 or 11, depending on the player's choice. At the start, each player and the dealer receive two cards; players' cards are dealt face up, while the dealer has one card face down (the "hole" card). Players can "hit" (take another card) or "stand" (keep their current hand) to improve their total. If a player's hand exceeds 21, they "bust" and lose automatically. The dealer must hit until their hand reaches at least 17. A "Blackjack" (Ace + 10-value card on the initial deal) is the strongest hand, automatically winning unless the dealer also has a Blackjack, resulting in a tie.
